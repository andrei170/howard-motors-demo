Our Story

A family opened a Monroe Road shop in 1976. It is still here.

Howard Motors opened at 3510 Monroe Road in De Pere, Wisconsin in 1976. The shop has been family-run since that first year, working on cars, trucks, and SUVs for De Pere drivers and selling used vehicles from the lot at the same address.

Forty-nine years later, the address has not moved and the family-run framing has not changed. That continuity is not an accident. Most independent mechanic shops do not clear ten years. Most family-run used-car lots do not clear twenty. Monroe Road has carried this one across both bars by a wide margin, with a service bay on one side and a sales lot on the other.

Foundation

Email Infrastructure on the Domain

MX, SPF, DKIM, DMARC + a real inbox · The channel that does not currently work

The howardmotorsindepere.com domain has no mail-exchange records on the live DNS as of the audit. A customer who emails sales@ or info@ on the domain gets a bounce. Stand up Google Workspace (or Microsoft 365), add MX records, configure SPF / DKIM / DMARC, route a real inbox to the counter, and surface the address on every page.

  • Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 provisioned on the domain
  • MX records added at the registrar
  • SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured for deliverability
  • Standard inboxes (sales, service, info) routed to the operator
